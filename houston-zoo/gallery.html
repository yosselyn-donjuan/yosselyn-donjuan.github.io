Animals and Exhibits

Here is a glimpse of some of the animals and habitats you might see during a visit to the Houston Zoo. Each area is designed with animal welfare and guest experience in mind.

Giraffes

Watch giraffes stretch for leafy snacks and interact with their herd in a sunny savanna habitat.

Lions

See lions relaxing in the shade or surveying their territory from rocky overlooks.

Elephants

Visit the elephant habitat to watch these gentle giants explore, play, and cool off in the water.

Zebras

Admire the bold stripes of zebras as they graze and move together across their habitat.

Aquarium

Colorful fish, coral, and other aquatic life create a peaceful underwater scene inside the aquarium.

Flamingos

Bright pink flamingos gather in shallow water, preening and wading together in groups.
