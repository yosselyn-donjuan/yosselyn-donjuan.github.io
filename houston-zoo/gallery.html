Animals and Exhibits

Here is a glimpse of some of the animals and habitats you might see during a visit to the Houston Zoo. Each area is designed with animal welfare and guest experience in mind.

Giraffes

Giraffes use their long necks to reach leaves high in the trees and watch over the savanna.

Lions

Lions rest in the shade or on rocks, keeping an eye on their habitat and their pride.

Elephants

Elephants use their trunks to eat, drink, and play, and they stay close to their herd.

Zebras

Zebras’ black and white stripes help them blend into the herd and confuse predators.

Aquarium

Bright fish and coral create an underwater world where guests can slow down and explore.

Flamingos

Flamingos stand on one leg and filter food from the water with their curved beaks.
