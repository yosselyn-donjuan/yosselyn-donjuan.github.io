Wildlife in the Heart of Houston

Discover animals, conservation, and unforgettable experiences.

The Houston Zoo is home to thousands of animals from all over the world. Walk shaded paths, watch your favorite species up close, and learn how everyday choices can help protect wildlife.

Where Every Visit Helps Save Animals

Every ticket, membership, and snack supports animal care and conservation projects around the globe. A day at the Houston Zoo is more than a fun outing—it is a chance to make a difference for wildlife.

From playful sea lions to towering giraffes, there is always something new to see. Bring friends, family, or just curiosity and explore a living classroom full of wonder.
