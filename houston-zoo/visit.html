Visit the Houston Zoo

The Houston Zoo is located inside Hermann Park, close to the Texas Medical Center and the Museum District. It is easy to reach by car, rideshare, or public transportation.

Parking and Accessibility

Parking is available in nearby lots and garages around Hermann Park. Arriving early on weekends and holidays is recommended, as spaces can fill quickly.

The zoo offers accessible pathways, ramps, and rest areas throughout the grounds. Wheelchair and stroller rentals are typically available near the entrance, and many exhibits are designed with clear viewing areas for all guests.
